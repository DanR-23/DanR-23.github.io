About us

Spices Indian Restaurant is located in Kingstanding and is a fullly lisenced, air conditioned restaurant with comforatable indoor and outdoor seating area. Customers get a welcoming feel upon entry as they are welcomed with well mannered polite staff ready to assist you on you experience with us.

Spices food is out of this world with some of the best dishes in birmingham, which will leave you wanting more. We are well known for our excelent dishes that you just cant beat such as our chicke tikka masala, chicken balti, chicken madras, and chicken vindaloo. We also offer amazing starters and sides that will make your experience with us perfect.

Spices has considered absolutely everything about your dining experience so that the decor and atmosphere perfectly complements the authentic and traditional menus – There is one note of caution though – due to Spices popularity it is advisable to book ahead – although you can rest assured that we will always do our very best to accommodate you at all times. So please feel free to visit us and enjoy dining at our relaxed and wonderfully friendly surroundings.
